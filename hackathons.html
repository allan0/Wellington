Python for AI Hackathon

About the Hackathon

Join us for the Python for AI Hackathon at Wellington Campus! This event is designed for aspiring coders and AI enthusiasts to test their skills, collaborate with peers, and create innovative AI solutions using Python. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced programmer, this hackathon offers a platform to learn, compete, and win exciting prizes.

Event Details

  • Date: April, 2025
  • Time: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Venue: Wellington Campus, Dubai, UAE

Prerequisites

  • Basic Python programming knowledge
  • Familiarity with AI concepts
  • Access to a laptop with necessary software installed (Python, Jupyter Notebook, etc.)

Hackathon Agenda

  • 10:00 AM: Registration & Welcome
  • 10:30 AM: Introduction to AI & Python Tools
  • 11:00 AM: Hackathon Begins
  • 1:00 PM: Lunch Break
  • 2:00 PM: Hackathon Continues
  • 5:00 PM: Project Presentations & Judging
  • 6:00 PM: Awards Ceremony & Closing

Prizes and Benefits

  • 1st Place: $1,000 + Internship Opportunity
  • 2nd Place: $500 + Free Course Enrollment
  • Certificates for All Participants
  • Networking with Industry Professionals
