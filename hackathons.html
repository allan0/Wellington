Support Structure

Wellington Campus provides comprehensive Day 1 support:

  • Challenge Briefing: Detailed briefs & Q&A.
  • Mentor Assignment: 1 per team (faculty/industry).
  • Help Desk: Technical & structuring guidance.
  • Pitch Coaching: Mid-day station for deck refinement.
  • Resource Access: Digital folder (datasets, tools, templates).
  • Marketing Support: Capturing event moments.

Day 1 Outcomes

By 2:00 PM, each team aims for:

  • Rough working model or defined logic (Excel/Python/UI).
  • Preliminary 3–5 slide pitch deck draft.
  • Submitted materials for internal review.

Challenge Themes

Your team will be assigned one of the following pre-defined industry challenge themes via the Spin the Wheel activity. Problem statements were shared in advance.

  • Supply Chain & Logistics
  • Sustainability & Environment
  • Medicine & Healthcare
  • Project Management
  • Real Estate & Urban Planning
  • Government & Public Sector
  • Open Innovation

You are encouraged to bring your own twist or solution model to the problem you're assigned!
